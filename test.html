Princess Shrine

THE VENUE

Of course, the bridal wear, the cake and the flowers are all important. But it’s the venue that packs the biggest style punch. Get inspired by these mesmerizing venues:

Princess Shrine

The Need to know :
Ceremony Capacity (Lawn): 700
Exclusivity: Yes
Reception Capacity: 1500
Accommodation: 4 + 2 suit rooms
Catering: Yes
Decor: In-house
The Setting :

Princess Shrine is a luxurious wedding venue located in the heart of Bangalore with MG road and Majestic being only a few kms away. With the green luscious lawn being a stone throw away, this allows for some awesome outdoor photos.

The Vibe :

Princess Shrine, a beautiful magnificent ground dating back to 2009, lends itself to you special day. This alluring venue with its majestic lawn has retained all its character and sense of tradition. The combination of an elegant historic setting and modern luxury makes this an incredibly versatile and stylish venue. Exclusive decor and caterer are available at the venue and offer a range of themes and menus which can be bespoke to the clients needs for their special day.
